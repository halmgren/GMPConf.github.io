OpenMR Benelux 2020, 22-23 January, Arnhem, The Netherlands

OpenMR Benelux 2020

OpenMR Benelux 2020 is a event on open MRI science, which will be held in Arnhem on the 22nd and 23th of January 2020.

The goal of the meeting is to discuss and disseminate practices and software in the scientific MRI world. The event will consist of talks concerning open science (with a strong emphasis on discussions), workshops on specific tools to make research open, and a hackathon (either in parallel or before/after the event). Although the event has an emphasize on MR (magnetic resonance) science, it will include topics on general scientific practices and meta-science as well.
