Program

Registration

  • 08:00 - 08:30, June 19, 2019 - Goldcorp Center
  • 08:30 - 09:00, June 20 & 21, 2019 - Harbour Center Concourse
  • There will be no on-site registration. Please register early.

Presentation slots are 25 minutes (20 minutes talk + 5 minutes Q&A). Confirmed speakers are in bold.

To presenters: Mac computers are available at the conference. If you use your own laptop, please bring necessary connectors for connecting the HDMI cable. Please contact chairs-gmp2019@eg.org if you have any questions or time constraints.
